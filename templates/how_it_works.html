How FundiConnect Works

Your journey to finding the perfect skilled worker or landing your next job starts here

1

Create Your Free Account

Sign up in less than 2 minutes. Choose whether you're a worker looking for jobs or a customer needing services. Provide your basic information and get started immediately.

Tip: Complete your profile to increase visibility
2

Build Your Profile (Workers)

Showcase your skills, experience, and portfolio. Add your hourly rate, location, and a professional description. The more complete your profile, the more customers will trust you.

Pro Tip: Get verified to earn the trusted badge
3

Post a Job or Browse Workers

Customers: Describe your job requirements, set a budget, and post it for free. Workers: Browse available jobs that match your skills and apply with a personalized message.

Tip: Use filters to find exactly what you need
4

Connect & Communicate

Review worker profiles, check their ratings, and compare bids. Message directly through our platform to discuss details, timelines, and expectations before hiring.

Tip: Ask for references or portfolio examples
5

Hire & Complete Work

Agree on terms and get the job done. For premium subscribers, we offer escrow protection for secure payments. Track progress and communicate throughout the project.

Tip: Use M-Pesa for secure transactions
6

Review & Rate

After job completion, leave an honest review. Your feedback helps build trust in the community and helps other customers make informed decisions.

Tip: Honest reviews help everyone
