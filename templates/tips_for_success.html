Tips for Success

Expert advice to help you stand out and grow on FundiConnect

Complete Your Profile

A complete profile gets 5x more views than incomplete ones. Add a professional photo, detailed description, and showcase your best work.

  • Add a clear profile picture
  • List all your skills and certifications
  • Include portfolio images of your best work

Get Verified

Verified workers receive 3x more job offers. The trusted badge builds instant credibility with potential customers.

  • Submit valid identification documents
  • Share professional certificates
  • Complete the verification process

Respond Quickly

Customers prefer workers who respond within minutes. Set up notifications and reply to messages promptly.

  • Enable push notifications
  • Set realistic response time expectations
  • Follow up within 24 hours

Collect Great Reviews

Positive reviews build your reputation. Deliver excellent service and ask satisfied customers to leave feedback.

  • Go above and beyond expectations
  • Communicate clearly throughout the job
  • Politely ask for reviews after completion

Price Competitively

Research market rates and price your services competitively. Consider offering package deals for repeat customers.

  • Check what others charge for similar services
  • Offer first-time customer discounts
  • Bundle services for better value

Upgrade to Premium

Premium subscribers get featured in search results and priority placement. The investment pays off with more job opportunities.

  • Unlimited job applications
  • Featured listing in search results
  • Verified badge eligibility

Do's and Don'ts for Workers

✓ Be professional in all communications
✗ Never ask for payment before work is done
✓ Show up on time or communicate delays
✗ Don't overpromise what you can deliver
✓ Keep customers updated on progress
✗ Don't ignore negative feedback - learn from it
✓ Ask clarifying questions before starting
✗ Don't share personal contact info before hiring

Tips for Customers

✓ Be clear about your requirements
✓ Check worker reviews and ratings
✓ Set realistic timelines and budgets
✓ Communicate expectations upfront
✓ Leave honest reviews to help others
✓ Use M-Pesa for secure payments
