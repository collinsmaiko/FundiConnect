Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade later?

Yes! You can upgrade to any premium plan at any time. Your subscription will be prorated.

How do I pay?

We accept M-Pesa Pochi La Biashara. After selecting a plan, you'll receive payment instructions.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, cancel anytime. Premium features remain until the end of your billing period.

What happens after expiration?

You'll revert to the Free plan. Your profile and job posts remain.

Is there a refund policy?

7-day money-back guarantee for all premium plans.

How do I get verified?

Verification is available for premium users. Submit your ID or professional certificate.
