Safety Tips

Your safety is our priority. Follow these guidelines for a secure experience

Verify Before Hiring

Always check worker profiles for verification badges, reviews, and ratings before hiring. Verified workers have undergone identity verification.

  • ✓ Look for the verified badge
  • ✓ Read recent reviews
  • ✓ Check their completed jobs count

Communicate on Platform

Keep all communication within FundiConnect. This helps us protect your information and provides a record of all discussions.

  • ✓ Use our messaging system
  • ✓ Don't share personal contact info
  • ✓ Report suspicious messages

Secure Payments

Always use M-Pesa through our platform for secure transactions. Never pay in cash without a receipt or proof of payment.

  • ✓ Use M-Pesa Paybill
  • ✓ Get receipt numbers
  • ✓ Never pay fully upfront

Meet in Safe Locations

For in-person jobs, meet in public spaces first or ensure someone knows your location. Trust your instincts.

  • ✓ Meet in public areas
  • ✓ Tell someone your plans
  • ✓ Trust your gut feeling

Get Everything in Writing

Document job details, timelines, and pricing. Clear agreements prevent misunderstandings.

  • ✓ Confirm scope of work
  • ✓ Agree on payment terms
  • ✓ Set clear deadlines

Report Suspicious Activity

If something feels wrong, report it immediately. Our team reviews all reports within 24 hours.

  • ✓ Report scam attempts
  • ✓ Flag inappropriate behavior
  • ✓ Contact support for help
